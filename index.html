Converging Reflections On Scripture and Society.

Samuel Rajadurai

Rev. Dr. S. Samuel Rajadurai is a presbyter, working in Thiruchirappalli-Thanjavur Diocese, Church of South India(CSI), stationed at CSI Ellis Memorial Church Dharapuram, Tirupur District, Tamilnadu. He has to his credit a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Master's in Philosophy and Religion. IN 1991, he completed his theological education for church ministry from Tamilnadu Theological Seminary (TTS), Madurai. After working at centre for Labour Education and Development(LEAD), Chennai, for a year, he joined the church as a minister in 1992. In 1998,, he joined his post-graduation in Theology in Social Analysis from TTS. In 2008, he rejoined TTS and was awarded with a D.Th. degree in 2014 by the Senate of Serampore College (University). He has the ability to decipher the signs of the times, explain the imminence of catastrophe and the redemptive involvement of God through his association with people's movements and the scripture for more than twenty five years. This book brings forth a prophetic comprehension to understand the problem of food insecurity in the Indian subcontinent.
